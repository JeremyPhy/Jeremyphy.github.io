6866 Markham Space Invaders



As a founding member of Markham Community Robotics Team, I contributed to a succcessful rookie season in FRC division.


I took part in the 2017-2018 season, working as a mechanical engineer and robot driver. We competed in two regional events: ONT District Ryerson University and ONT District Durham College.

At Ryerson University, we had a tough start and ranked 34th overall with a record of 4-11-0. At Durham College, we ranked 19th overall with a record of 7-5-0 and qualified for our first ever playoff appearance. This led to a hard-fought quarterfinal finish.
