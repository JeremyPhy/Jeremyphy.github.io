6866 Markham Space Invaders



I was a founding member of Markham Community Robotics and helped them to a succcessful rookie season in FRC. As a mechanical engineer and robot driver, I contributed to hard-fought quarterfinal finish.

I was a member of the team in the 2017-2018 season and we attended two regional events: ONT District Ryerson University attended and ONT District Durham College. At Ryerson University, we had a tough start and ranked 34th overall with a record of 4-11-0. At Durham College, we ranked 19th overall with a record of 7-5-0 and qualified for our first ever playoff appearance.
